EXPERIENCE
In 2019, I performed ‘umrah’ with my family. This is my first experience abroad. I performed ‘umrah’ for two weeks. My first destination was Mecca and then after 8 days, my family and I went to Medina. After 7 days in Medina, we returned to Malaysia. As long as I was there, I was very happy and focused on performing my ‘umrah’. My parents taught me a lot how to perform worship perfectly. This is the best experience in my life because it is my first experience abroad by plane. I am very happy and grateful to have the opportunity to perform ‘umrah’ at the age of 18.
